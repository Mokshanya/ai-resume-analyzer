How to Upload Your Resume

1

Prepare your Document

Ensure your resume is saved as either a .PDF or a .DOCX format. We strictly reject image files (.jpg, .png) to guarantee high-precision text parsing.

2

Drag & Drop

Scroll down to the 'Upload' section on the main application. You can physically drag your file over the dashed zone, or click the "Browse Files" button to select it from your computer.

3

Interact with the Analyzed View

Instantly after uploading, your text is structurally divided into an interactive split-pane interface. Scroll through your "Initial Draft" on the left and see the heuristically optimized version glowing on the right!
